Morphbot

Morphbot's vision is to create robotic educational kits to provide students from K-12 to promote engineering and STEM curriculums at an earlier age. To faciliate this vision, Morphbot wanted to create self-assembling modular cubes that could arrange themselves to whatever the user chooses. The cubes were designed in Autodesk Fusion 360 and then rapid prototyped with 3D printers. They are controlled using a centralized system to allow for real-time communication between the user and each cube. The cubes themselves are assembled and tested to determine the accuracy of the components used. I was in charge of designing, assembling and testing each and every cube to determine the validity of the design and components used.
