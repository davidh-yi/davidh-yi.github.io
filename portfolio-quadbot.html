Robotic Studio

This is a four legged robot that can walk and dance; it was created, designed and assembled to dance in beat with music. The process included sketching a prototype robot, designing and assembling in CAD software, and finally prototyping and assembling using 3D printers, laser cutters, turning machines and soldering. The electronics consist of a Raspberry Pi, several servo motors, a DC converter, battery, and servo controller. This was a semester long project where I worked with one other person. We also created a video to showcase our robot's movements and a report to describe it.

Skills:

Hardware: 3D printing, CAD software, soldering, rapid prototyping, laser cutting, turning machine

Software: Python
